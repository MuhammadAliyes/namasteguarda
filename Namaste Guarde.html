History of the Namaste Guarde

Namaste Guarde is one of the best places in the city & the legendary taste of our specially prepared dishes still dominates the city. We offer several options. We welcome you to come relax, socialize and share good times with your family & friends at Indian Garden or order online and enjoy delicious food from the comfort of home.

Namaste Guarde serves freshly prepared authentic dishes and we believe that taste is all about the balance between sweet, sour, hot and spicy food. We invite you to experience the taste of Indian Garden and hope that you never forget the taste of our mouth-watering authentic dishes and the overall experience at our restaurant.
